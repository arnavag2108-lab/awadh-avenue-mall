About the Mall

Built for a city that's ready for it

Awadh Avenue Mall brings Barabanki its first organised retail, dining and entertainment destination — a single address for fashion, electronics, food and family outings that once meant a trip out of town.

Spread across 4 floors with a dedicated food court and a multiplex, the mall is designed to be Barabanki's everyday gathering place.
